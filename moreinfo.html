About Me!


Hello! My name is Caroline Pickering and I'm 16 years old. I'm a barista at Starbucks and also a participant of the SIP at Girls Who Code. I've lived in Florida, Washington, and Georgia. I love to cook and read, so I always want a good recipe or book reccomendation.

I love hiking, so any weekend I'm not working or doing homework, you can probably find me on a train in North Georgia. I've hiked parts of the Appalachian Trail and Pacific Crest Trail. I've hiked in almost every state I've been to, from Maine to Florida, from Washington to California. I would love to hike Mount Rainier in Washington State one day.
